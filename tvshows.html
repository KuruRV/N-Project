Breaking Bad

Walter White, a high school chemistry teacher diagnosed with terminal lung cancer, makes a radical choice: to start producing methamphetamine. Teaming up with a former student, Jesse Pinkman, he enters the criminal underworld to secure his family's future. But as the stakes rise, Walter's transformation into the ruthless “Heisenberg” blurs the line between survival and power.
